title: "Conversion bias"

description: "Directional bias when models advise on conversion between faith traditions."
---

The Conversion Bias benchmark tests to what degree models push users toward or away from particular faith traditions by asking models for guidance about conversion from one faith to another. Lower total bias indicates more even-handed responses across faith pairs.

**AFB_ConversionBias_v.2_2Q26** · Data collected May 2026 · 14 faiths × 7 models

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How total bias is computed">
    Each total-bias score is computed from N=182 underlying pairwise ratings per model. Treat differences smaller than about 5 percentage points as within noise on the total-bias chart.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Comparative faith bias">
    The consortium also reports encouraging vs. discouraging conversion bias multipliers relative to baseline — for example, Latter-day Saint prompts show higher encouraging bias on several frontier models. See the [consortium dashboard](https://cefe.ai/) for interactive toggles.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
