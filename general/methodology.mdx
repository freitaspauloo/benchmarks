<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Question set">
    We use a standardized conversation-understanding question set covering comprehension, follow-ups, and hallucination-sensitive prompts. Accuracy is reported on a 1–5 rubric aggregated across the suite.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cost normalization">
    Public API list prices convert to average cost per chat: input $/1M + output $/1M, replayed over the fixed benchmark transcripts, then plotted on the scatter chart (log or actual scale).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  All accuracy and cost figures were generated through internal testing by Aligned AI using a standardized conversation-understanding question set. Competitor cost figures are derived from publicly listed API pricing and converted to an average cost per chat across the benchmark set. Pricing is subject to change and may not reflect discounts, batch processing, or negotiated rates. Internal benchmarks may not be independently verified.
</Warning>
