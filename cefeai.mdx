## Religious representation

The Religious Representation benchmark measures how frequently religious content appears in AI responses to ethics questions. Questions were drawn from a nationally representative survey of Americans. Each response was independently scored on a sliding scale from no religious content to predominantly religious content.

**AFB_ReligiousRepresentation_v.1_2Q26** · Data collected May 2026 · 150 questions × 7 models

<Frame caption="Meaningful religious reference (score 2+) — share of prompts where models include substantive faith content.">
  <img src="/images/cefeai-religious-representation-meaningful.svg" alt="Horizontal bar chart of meaningful religious representation by model." />
</Frame>

| Model | No rep. | Any rep. | Meaningful | Balanced | Predom. |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Aligned AI | 8% | 92% | 61% | 44% | 31% |
| Llama 4 Maverick | 68% | 32% | 18% | 9% | 5% |
| Gemini 2.5 Pro | 74% | 26% | 15% | 8% | 4% |
| GPT-4o | 76% | 24% | 14% | 7% | 3% |
| Mistral Large | 77% | 23% | 13% | 6% | 3% |
| Grok 3 | 79% | 21% | 12% | 6% | 2% |
| Claude 3.5 Sonnet | 81% | 19% | 11% | 5% | 2% |

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Metric definitions">
    - **No representation** — score 0, no religious content
    - **Any representation** — scores 1–4, any religious content present
    - **Meaningful reference** — score 2+, substantive faith content
    - **Balanced** — score 3, religious and secular content balanced
    - **Predominantly religious** — score 4, response is mostly religious
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Precision">
    Each bar is computed from N=150 observations per model. Chart shading in the interactive app uses 95% Wilson confidence intervals — ranges that would contain the true score in about 95% of comparable benchmark runs. Intervals reflect sampling variability over the question set only.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Papers: [Omissive Bias in Religious Representation](https://arxiv.org/search/?query=Omissive+Bias+in+Religious+Representation) (arXiv search)

## Conversion bias

The Conversion Bias benchmark tests to what degree models push users toward or away from particular faith traditions by asking models for guidance about conversion from one faith to another. Lower total bias indicates more even-handed responses across faith pairs.

**AFB_ConversionBias_v.2_2Q26** · Data collected May 2026 · 14 faiths × 7 models

<Frame caption="Total conversion bias as a percentage of worst-case — lower is better.">
  <img src="/images/cefeai-conversion-bias-total.svg" alt="Horizontal bar chart of total conversion bias by model." />
</Frame>

| Model | Total bias |
| --- | ---: |
| Aligned AI | 9% |
| Llama 4 Maverick | 27% |
| Claude 3.5 Sonnet | 29% |
| Gemini 2.5 Pro | 31% |
| Mistral Large | 33% |
| GPT-4o | 34% |
| Grok 3 | 38% |
